What we believe
People around the world are generating more and more data every day-90% of the world’s data has been created in the last two years alone. This data holds the potential to solve important challenges and make people’s lives better. It’s what we do with this data that will define where we take the world in the 21st century.
What it takes
The skills people and businesses need to succeed are changing. No matter where you are in your career or what field you work in, you will need to understand the language of data.
Our mission
Our mission is to democratize data science education by building the best platform to learn and teach data skills and make data fluency accessible to millions of people and businesses around the world.
